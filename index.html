About Me

The Person Behind
The Blog

I'm MD Ratul Hossen, a Computer Science student at Daffodil International University, Dhaka. My obsession? Large Language Models — understanding how they work from the ground up, deploying them locally, and one day building scalable LLMs from scratch.

Beyond the technical, I write about life as a student in Bangladesh, the discipline I've learned from chess, and occasional poetry that refuses to stay in my head. This blog is my lab notebook, my journal, and my contribution to the community — all in one.
